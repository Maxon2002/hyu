At HYU Korean Restaurant, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.
What information we collect
When you make a reservation through our website, we collect:
  • Your name
  • Phone number

How we use your information
We use your information only to:

How we store your information
Reservation details are sent securely to our restaurant email and are not stored on the website. We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except as necessary to provide the reservation service.

You can contact us at any time to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information.
